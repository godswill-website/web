About us

Grace Community Church has been serving the local community since 1990.
Our mission is to share the teachings of Jesus christ and support our
community through outreach programs.

Our ministry

Children's ministry

Use the bible to show how God lives in us through the holy spirit
and is there to guide us

Emphasize God's love unconditional love and how his grace works in our lives.

Use a "choice" game to help children make decisions between being kind or unkind, and
discuss how the right choices bring us closer to God.

Youth Ministry

Engage youth in meaningful service projects, like helping at a local soup kitchen.
or starting a community garden. Partner with local schools or other organizations to create volunter opportunities.

Adult Bible Study

Explore topics that connect faith to daily concerns, such as work, relationships, finances, mental, health, and decision-making. Study biblical virtues like the fruit of the spirit(love, joy, peace, patience). or forgiveness to encourage personal growth.

Community Outreach

Raising awareness about specific issues, resources, or services available to community members. Education: Providing educational programs, workshops, or seminars to enhance knowledge and skills.
